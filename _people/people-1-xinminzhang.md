<br/>Xinmin Zhang was born in Jiujiang, China, in November 1990. Currently, he is an Assistant Professor in the School of Public Administration, Jiangxi University of Finance and Economics, China. Previously, he was a Visiting Scholar at Xi'an Jiaotong University. His current research interests include landscape ecology, ecological security, climate change and environmental pollution. He has approximately 80 refereed papers published in SCI and SSCI journals, with a total number of citations of more than 1900 times and an H-index of 24. His work has been published in _Nature Communications_, _Cities_, _Sustainable Cities and Society_, _Renewable Energy_, _Environmental Impact Assessment Review_, _Journal of Cleaner Production_, _Science of the Total Environment_, etc.<br/>

## Academic Experience

* 2019.4—Now, Assistant Professor, Jiangxi University of Finance and Economics, China
